Description

Popcorn is my go-to when I need a smaller dinner. It's also my comfort food when I'm not feeling great.

What You'll Need

  • Popcorn kernals
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Coconut oil

What to Do

  1. Put 1-2 tbsp of coconut oil in a pot over medium heat
  2. Put 2 popcorn kernals in the oil and wait until they pop. Once they pop, you know the oil is hot enough.
  3. Pour in 1-2 cups of popcorn kernals and cover the pot
  4. Melt butter while popcorn is popping
  5. Once popping slows, take the pot off the stove
  6. Pour popcorn into a serving bowl and butter and salt to taste
  7. Bon appetit
