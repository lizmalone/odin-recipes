Description:

Bite into a delicious homemade pepperoni pizza with only a couple of easy ingredients!

What you'll need:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices

What to Do

  1. Prehead the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Make the pizza dough
  3. Toss the pizza dough
  4. Spread pizza sauce on the dough
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the pizza sauce
  6. Top it off with slices of pepperoni
  7. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes
  8. Bon appetit!
